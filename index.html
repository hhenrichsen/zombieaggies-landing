Day Play

This year our game will have two portions, just like normal. The first is Day Play. This will look a little different than normal.

A map on the website will update every 30 minutes with new locations available where QR codes can be scanned for points. Players will be randomly assigned to the Human or Zombie teams, and will compete among themselves as well as with non-players. Different points will give different resources that are worth different point amounts for each team. You will be able to scan each QR code every five minutes. We're using Point Control in order to make this easy for everyone.

Night Missions

Night play will be a lot closer to what you'd expect: games where two teams compete for some objective in order to earn the most points. Rules for night play will be released a few hours before the missions start. You'll have to sign in each night in order to receive a bandanna and participate in the mission.

Rules Listing

Ground Rules

  1. If you are experiencing COVID symptoms, you are not allowed to participate in in-person activities.
  2. Follow social distancing guidelines at all times.
  3. Treat other players, moderators, and admins with respect and sportspersonship.

Day Play Rules

  1. Follow the ground rules and use common sense.
  2. Do not interfere with classes, or scan codes during class.
  3. Do not block locations where codes can be found or otherwise interfere with their scanning.
  4. Follow moderator instructions.

Night Play Rules

  1. Follow the ground rules and use common sense.
  2. These will vary by the activity.

Zero Tolerance Rules

  1. Disrespect towards players/moderators/admins
  2. Excessive profanity/rude gestures
  3. Unsportspersonlike conduct
  4. Excessive conflict during an argument
  5. Violent or threatening behavior
  6. Unsafe activity
